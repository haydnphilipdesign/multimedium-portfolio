# Starter Demo Image Plan

Use these filenames when sourcing images for the Starter showcase demo.

## Required images

| Filename | Placement | What to source | Target size |
|---|---|---|---|
| `hero-home-office.webp` | Home hero | Professional woman at a tidy home-office desk with laptop, paperwork, and natural light. Personal, calm, approachable. | 1200x900 |
| `hero-home-office.jpg` | Home hero fallback | JPG fallback for the same image above. | 1200x900 |
| `sarah-headshot.webp` | About section | Friendly professional woman, natural lighting, approachable expression, simple wardrobe. | 760x1014 |
| `sarah-headshot.jpg` | About section fallback | JPG fallback for the same headshot. | 760x1014 |

## Tone notes

- Starter should feel personal, dependable, and close to the day-to-day reality of a solo TC.
- Avoid glossy corporate team photos.
- Prioritize warmth over luxury.

## Sourcing shortlist

Verified March 24, 2026.

### `hero-home-office.webp` / `hero-home-office.jpg`

Primary pick:

- [Woman working on laptop at home](https://www.pexels.com/photo/woman-working-on-laptop-at-home-12912111/) - Strongest match for the brief. Natural light, visible papers, and a lived-in home-office feel that reads personal rather than corporate.

Alternates:

- [A woman working on her desk](https://www.pexels.com/photo/a-woman-working-on-her-desk-6694861/) - Slightly more polished/styled, but still warm and believable. Good fallback if the primary crop feels too busy.
- [Woman working on laptop by white desk](https://www.pexels.com/photo/woman-working-on-laptop-by-white-desk-7718745/) - Clean and tidy with a calm tone, though it reads a bit more editorial and less paperwork-heavy.

Crop notes:

- Target crop is 4:3 at 1200x900.
- Keep the laptop, hands, and at least one paper/notebook in frame so the service reads as operational support, not generic remote work.
- Avoid crops that make the office feel too design-forward or luxury-coded.

### `sarah-headshot.webp` / `sarah-headshot.jpg`

Primary pick:

- [Confident professional woman smiling indoors](https://www.pexels.com/photo/confident-professional-woman-smiling-indoors-27086864/) - Warm, approachable, and businesslike without feeling stiff. Best fit for "friendly professional woman" in the current demo copy.

Alternates:

- [Portrait of female entrepreneur in natural light](https://www.pexels.com/photo/portrait-of-female-entrepreneur-in-natural-light-33867528/) - Softer and more lifestyle-oriented. Strong option if we want Sarah to feel more boutique and personal.
- [Woman smiling indoors with natural light](https://www.pexels.com/photo/woman-smiling-indoors-with-natural-light-33867557/) - Bright and welcoming, though it leans a little more personal-branding than transaction-coordinator.

Crop notes:

- Target crop is 380:507, exported at 760x1014.
- Keep the eyes in the upper third and preserve some shoulder/torso so it still feels like a service-business portrait, not a beauty close-up.
- Favor simple wardrobe and uncluttered background details.

## License note

- Pexels says its photos can be used on websites, blogs, and apps under the [Pexels License](https://www.pexels.com/license/).
- Even with broad stock licenses, avoid visible trademarks, copyrighted artwork, or anything that makes the image feel editorial instead of commercial.

## Recommendation

- If we want the fastest path, use the two primary picks above.
- If we want the hero and headshot to feel like the same real person, stock will always be a compromise; a custom mini-shoot would be noticeably stronger than trying to force continuity from separate stock images.
