What I Help With

Reliable transaction support that makes you look organized and responsive.

Once a file goes under contract, I step in to manage the details that can eat up your day: document follow-up, deadline tracking, communication with title and lenders, and all the little reminders that keep a closing from getting messy.

This service is built for agents who want one dependable person in their corner, not a giant operation with rotating contacts. You always know who is handling your file, and I always know what matters most to your business.

About Sarah

I built Summit to be the kind of support I know agents actually need.

I started Summit Transaction Services after seeing how often good agents were pulled away from client relationships by follow-up emails, paperwork, and deadline management. I wanted to offer calm, dependable help that made their business feel more steady.

My style is personal, organized, and responsive. I care about the experience behind the scenes just as much as the closing day itself, because that is what helps agents stay confident and clients feel taken care of.
