What We Do

Transaction Coordination from Contract to Close

Summit Transaction Services manages every step of the transaction process after a deal goes under contract. We keep paperwork organized, deadlines on track, and communication flowing between all parties involved.

This allows agents and investors to focus on negotiations, client relationships, and closing more deals — while we handle the logistics behind the scenes.

  • Buyers & Sellers
  • Title & Escrow Companies
  • Lenders
  • Attorneys
  • Inspectors & Appraisers
