What We Do

Clearpath becomes the operational layer behind your closings.

We step in once a deal is signed and immediately create structure around it: file setup, deadline mapping, communication cadence, and follow-up with the people who keep a closing moving. The goal is not just getting to the finish line. It is getting there in a way that feels organized to everyone involved.

That makes Clearpath a strong fit for agents who have grown past “winging it” but do not want the overhead of building a full in-house operations department yet.

Typical Clearpath clients

  • Solo agents with steady monthly volume
  • Small teams ready for a cleaner process
  • TC businesses supporting 10 to 20+ active agents
  • Operators who need a polished submission portal
