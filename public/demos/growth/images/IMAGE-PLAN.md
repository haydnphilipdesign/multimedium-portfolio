# Growth Demo Image Plan

Use these filenames when sourcing images for the Growth showcase demo.

## Required images

| Filename | Placement | What to source | Target size |
|---|---|---|---|
| `hero-operations-office.webp` | Home hero stage | Modern office scene with a professional reviewing documents or a tablet. Polished and organized, not luxury-staged. | 1200x900 |
| `hero-operations-office.jpg` | Home hero fallback | JPG fallback for the same image above. | 1200x900 |
| `maria-headshot.webp` | Home about section | Business-professional woman with confident, approachable energy. | 760x1014 |
| `maria-headshot.jpg` | Home about section fallback | JPG fallback for the same headshot. | 760x1014 |
| `services-workflow.webp` | Services feature split | Professional at a desk reviewing checklists, contracts, or a document dashboard. | 1200x900 |
| `services-workflow.jpg` | Services feature split fallback | JPG fallback for the same image above. | 1200x900 |

## Tone notes

- Growth should feel established, systematic, and polished.
- Favor organized office and workflow imagery over lifestyle shots.
- The impression should be “this business has a process.”
