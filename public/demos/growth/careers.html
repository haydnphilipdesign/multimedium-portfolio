Transaction Coordinator (Contract)

Clearpath Closings is looking for a contract transaction coordinator to support our growing team. In this role, you'll manage real estate files from contract to close, ensuring deadlines are met, documents are organized, and all parties stay informed throughout the process.

This is a remote, contract-based position ideal for someone with experience in real estate transactions or a strong administrative background looking to specialize in transaction coordination.

About the Role

You'll be responsible for managing the full lifecycle of real estate transactions — from executed contract to closing. This includes tracking deadlines, coordinating with title companies, lenders, and agents, preparing documents, and ensuring every file meets compliance standards. You'll work directly with Maria Torres and have the flexibility to manage your own schedule.

What We Value

Attention to detail, clear communication, reliability, and a proactive approach to problem-solving. Experience with real estate transactions is preferred but not required — we'll provide training and systems to set you up for success.
