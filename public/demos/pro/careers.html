Join the Apex TC Team

We’re building a team of detail-oriented professionals who are passionate about real estate and client success.

Apex TC is growing, and we’re looking for talented individuals who thrive on organization, clear communication, and getting things done right. If you’re detail-oriented and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment, we’d love to hear from you.

Full-Time · Remote

Transaction Coordinator (Contract)

We’re looking for an experienced transaction coordinator to join our growing team. You’ll manage files from contract to close, communicate with all transaction parties, and ensure every deadline is met with precision.

The ideal candidate has at least one year of TC or real estate administrative experience, is comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, and takes pride in accuracy and proactive communication.

What You’ll Do

Manage 20–30 active transaction files, coordinate with agents, lenders, title companies, and all parties involved. Track deadlines, review documents for compliance, and deliver organized files at closing.

Requirements

1+ years TC or real estate admin experience. Strong attention to detail. Proficiency with transaction management platforms. Excellent written communication.

Operations Assistant (Part-Time)

We’re hiring a part-time operations assistant to support our coordination team. This role focuses on administrative tasks, document preparation, and ensuring our systems and files stay organized and up to date.
