Founder

Meet Jordan Blake

Jordan Blake founded Apex TC after years of working in the real estate industry and seeing firsthand how much time agents and investors lose to transaction paperwork, follow-ups, and compliance requirements.

With a background in operations and a deep understanding of the real estate closing process, Jordan built Apex TC to be the kind of coordination partner every agent deserves — detail-oriented, proactive, and always a step ahead.

Today, Apex TC has grown from a solo operation into a dedicated team of coordinators serving agents, teams, and investors across multiple states. Every file we manage reflects our commitment to accuracy, transparency, and client success.
