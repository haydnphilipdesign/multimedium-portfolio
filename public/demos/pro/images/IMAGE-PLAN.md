# Pro Demo Image Plan

Use these filenames when sourcing images for the Pro showcase demo.

## Required images

| Filename | Placement | What to source | Target size |
|---|---|---|---|
| `hero-team-collaboration.webp` | Home hero or service imagery | Premium team collaboration scene around a conference table or strategy session. Should feel authoritative and polished. | 1200x900 |
| `jordan-headshot.webp` | About page founder section | Confident professional portrait, premium but approachable. Gender-neutral styling works best. | 760x1014 |
| `services-team-meeting.webp` | Services feature split | Team reviewing dashboards, documents, or workflow plans in a high-end office setting. | 1200x900 |

## Tone notes

- Pro should feel premium, team-oriented, and authority-led.
- Prefer real collaboration imagery over generic handshake photos.
- The impression should be “this team can support serious volume.”
