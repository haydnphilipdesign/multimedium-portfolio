# SEO Strategy

## Core Direction
MULTIMEDIUM should compete on niche relevance, credibility, clean technical SEO, and useful proof content, not on broad vanity terms like “web design” by themselves.

## Target Keyword Clusters
| Cluster | Primary pages | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Real estate website design | `/industries/real-estate-professionals`, `/services/website`, `/work` | Core commercial cluster for agents, teams, and brokerages |
| Transaction coordinator website design | `/industries/transaction-coordinators`, `/tc-packages`, `/resources` | Strongest niche specialization cluster on the site |
| Website design for real estate coaches | `/industries/real-estate-coaches`, `/services/landing-pages`, `/work?industry=coaching` | Premium authority / offer-driven cluster |
| Real estate marketing websites | `/services/landing-pages`, `/services/growth-retainers`, relevant case studies | Good supporting cluster for conversion-focused pages |
| Local web design relevance | homepage, about, contact, select industry pages | Keep this truthful and tied to real service areas, not fake city spam |

## Recommended Page Structure
- Homepage: broad niche positioning and strong internal links into the main money pages.
- Services hub: supports commercial intent for the actual offer stack.
- Industry pages:
  - Real Estate Professionals
  - Transaction Coordinators
  - Real Estate Coaches
  - Home Services
  - Trades
  - HOAs
- Proof layer:
  - work archive
  - individual case studies
- Resource layer:
  - transaction coordinator templates and PDFs

## Internal Linking Recommendations
- Homepage should keep linking prominently to:
  - `/industries/real-estate-professionals`
  - `/industries/transaction-coordinators`
  - `/industries/real-estate-coaches`
- Service pages should keep pointing to the most relevant industry pages, not just back to the services hub.
- Resource pages should continue linking back to the transaction coordinator money page and TC packages page.
- Case studies should keep acting as proof destinations from industry and service pages.
- Footer should continue reinforcing the highest-value niche routes rather than generic pages only.

## Local SEO Recommendations
- Use genuine local relevance only.
- Prioritize:
  - Poconos
  - Northeastern Pennsylvania
  - Pennsylvania
- Only create dedicated local service pages if you can support them with real proof, real work, or real market relevance.
- Good future local pages if justified:
  - `Web Design for Real Estate Professionals in the Poconos`
  - `Transaction Coordinator Website Design in Pennsylvania`
- Avoid city-page proliferation unless you actually have market proof there.

## Conversion Recommendations
- Keep every important page focused on one primary next step.
- For real estate pages: quote request, message, or call.
- For coach/brokerage pages: strategy call, inquiry, or landing page planning.
- For TC pages: booked discovery call or package inquiry.

## Link Building / Off-Page Direction
- Build proof and citations around niches, not generic design directories.
- Better off-page targets:
  - real estate operations communities
  - TC communities and podcasts
  - local business directories with real geographic relevance
  - guest posts or collaborations with real estate coaches or brokerages

## What Not To Do
- No fake city pages.
- No generic “top 10 SEO tips” filler content.
- No duplicate service pages targeting tiny keyword variants.
- No manufactured testimonials or fake local presence claims.
