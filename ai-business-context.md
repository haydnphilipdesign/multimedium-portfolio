## TC Website Packages

These are productized website packages built specifically for transaction coordinators. Three tiers designed around how TC businesses actually work.

### TC Starter — $750
- **Pages:** 1 (single-page scrollable)
- **Timeline:** 1–2 weeks
- **Best for:** New solo TCs who need credibility and a professional web presence from day one
- **Includes:**
  - Hero, services, about, and contact sections
  - Mobile-responsive design
  - SEO-ready meta tags
  - Template-guided copy (client provides details, I adapt the structure)
  - Custom color scheme from their branding
  - Scroll animations

### TC Growth — $1,500 (Most Popular)
- **Pages:** 4 (Home, Services, Submit a File, Careers)
- **Timeline:** 3–4 weeks
- **Best for:** Established TCs who want stronger branding, a real intake workflow, and room to grow
- **Includes:**
  - Embedded intake forms (Cognito, Jotform, etc.)
  - Strategic messaging and positioning
  - Benefit cards, process steps, differentiators
  - Scroll animations and polished interactions
  - Collaborative copywriting (I write, they review)
  - Hamburger mobile menu

### TC Pro — $3,000+
- **Pages:** 5+ (everything in Growth plus custom additions)
- **Timeline:** 4–6 weeks
- **Best for:** TC teams, coaches, or operators who want their website to actively generate business
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Growth, plus custom pages
  - Testimonials and social proof sections
  - Referral partner landing pages
  - Advanced intake with multi-step forms
  - Fully written copy and brand positioning
  - First year of managed hosting included ($59/mo after)
  - Deep discovery call, multiple copy review rounds
  - Analytics setup (GA4)

### What Every Package Includes
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Custom branding (colors, logo, identity)
- SEO fundamentals (meta tags, Open Graph, structured markup)
- Fast page speed (clean code, no framework bloat)
- Copy guidance or writing (at the tier's level)
- Launch support (domain setup, DNS, going live)

### Upgrade Path
All packages are built on the same design system, so upgrading from Starter to Growth or Growth to Pro doesn't mean starting over. Branding, colors, and content carry forward.

### Technical Stack for TC Packages
- Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks or page builders
- Fast, portable, easy to host anywhere
